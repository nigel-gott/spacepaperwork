2021/05/02 Personal Items

Items can now be created in {{ SITE_NAME }} without first making a fleet.

Visit here to do so or find it under the newly renamed Items menu and then click Your Items. Items created this way are owned 100% by the character you select when adding the items. Otherwise they work just like items added using a fleet, so you can still contract them to a seller or sell them yourselves and you will get 100% of the profit.
