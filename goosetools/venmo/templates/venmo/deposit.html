Deposit Eggs

To deposit eggs (turn your in-game isk into eggs) you must:

  1. Donate the isk you want to turn into eggs to any of the in-game corps (MEAN,HONK,XBUX,HAUS,GOOP).
  2. Take a screenshot of the donation making sure the quantity, corp and time of donation are visible.
  3. Upload the screenshot to an image sharing website like imgur.
  4. Paste a link to the image in the notes section below.
  5. Enter the same quantity in the field below.
  6. Click submit and wait for approval from an admin who will check the donation.
